✦ Phase 1 · Section 1 · Lesson 3
Buyers vs Sellers
This is the lesson where it clicks. Every single candle is a battle. Your job is not to guess. Your job is to see who is in control and stop overcomplicating what the chart is saying.
~12 min Highly interactive +55 XP
Core idea
Price moves because buyers and sellers are fighting for control.
Buyers think price should go higher. Sellers think price should go lower.

When buyers are stronger, price lifts. When sellers are stronger, price drops. That’s what candles are showing you in real time — who won that moment.
Teach → Experience
What do buyers and sellers actually do?
Before you can identify who is in control, you first need to understand what each side does to price.

Buyers push price higher. Sellers push price lower. Your job is to stop seeing candles as random and start seeing them as pressure.
🟢
Buyers
They are willing to step in and pay more. When buyers are stronger, price tends to lift.
🔴
Sellers
They are willing to accept less to get out. When sellers are stronger, price tends to drop.
Sellers push down Pressure Buyers push up
Tap a side and watch what it does to price.
Why this matters
You are not being asked to guess control yet. First, you are learning the basic language: buyers create upward pressure, sellers create downward pressure. Once that is clear, then chart reading starts making sense.
Experience
Push the market meter
Tap buyers or sellers to see what happens when one side starts getting stronger than the other. This is a simple feel exercise — not theory.

Teaching moment
The body matters more than the drama.
Long wicks can look dramatic, but the body tells you where price actually settled. That’s why later in ICC, close beats wick. You’re training your eyes for that right now.
Correct a misconception
Control vs prediction
A lot of beginners think “buyers are in control” means “price can never pull back.” That’s not true. Control tells you who currently has the upper hand — not that price will move in a straight line forever.
✅
Better understanding
Buyers are in control right now, so I want to look for opportunities on their side unless that control clearly changes.
❌
Bad assumption
Buyers are in control, so price will never dip and I should just chase anything green immediately.

What you know now
📈
Buyers push price up. If they are stronger, candles tend to close higher.
📉
Sellers push price down. If they are stronger, candles tend to close lower.
🕯️
Candles are behavior. They are showing you who won that period.
🎯
Control matters more than opinions. You trade with the side that is clearly showing strength.
