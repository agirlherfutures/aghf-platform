Candle Anatomy

Now that you know candles tell a story, we need to break down the body parts: open, high, low, close, body, and wick. These pieces show how price moved and where buyers or sellers reacted.

~16 minAnatomy lesson+95 XP
Core idea

Every candle has body parts.

The candle body shows the distance between the open and close. The wick shows where price traveled but did not fully hold. The high and low show the full range of that candle.

Body vs wick

What is the body showing you?

The body is the thick part of the candle. It shows the distance between where price opened and where price closed.

Wick behavior

What does a wick show?

A wick shows where price traveled but did not fully hold.

Wick = traveled but not held
Reinforce
A wick does not automatically mean reversal. It means price reached that area and did not close there. Context tells you what that rejection means.

What you know now

📍
Open and close create the candle body. The body shows where price started and finished.
📈
High and low show the full range. They tell you how far price traveled during that candle.
🕯️
Wicks show travel without full acceptance. Price reached there, but did not close there.
🧠
The close matters. A wick is not the same thing as a candle closure.
✦ Dayli note
