Position Sizing

Position sizing is how much you choose to trade. Same setup, same chart, same stop loss — but the size you choose changes the money risk completely. This lesson teaches you to control size before size controls you.

~17 minRisk control lesson+105 XP
Core idea

Contracts change the money, not the structure.

Your entry can be clean and your stop loss can be correct, but if your size is too big, the trade becomes emotionally heavy. Position sizing is what makes the same setup feel manageable or reckless.

Teach → Experience

What you know now
