Stop Loss & Take Profit

Entries don’t make traders. Exits do. In your framework, stop loss is based on structure only: below the swing low in an uptrend, above the swing high in a downtrend. This lesson teaches protection, intention, and discipline.

~18 minRisk lesson+100 XP
Core idea

Your stop loss belongs where the setup is proven wrong.

That means in an uptrend, the stop loss belongs below the swing low. In a downtrend, it belongs above the swing high. Not random points. Not fear. Structure only.
