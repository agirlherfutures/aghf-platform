✦ Section 2 · Lesson 9

Brokers & Prop Firms

You don’t just open a chart and magically trade. There is always a middle step: you either trade with your own money through a broker, or you trade with someone else’s capital through a prop firm.

~15 minReal-world lesson+90 XP
Core idea

You either trade your money… or someone else’s.

With a broker, you use your own money, keep your profits, and take your own losses. With a prop firm, you trade under rules using access to their capital, and profits are usually split.

What you know now

💰
Brokers use your money. You own the gains and the losses directly.
🏢
Prop firms use rules plus capital access. You do not get to ignore rules just because you made money.
⚖️
More capital does not mean more freedom. It often means more responsibility and tighter discipline.
🧠
Your entry path matters. Beginners need to understand how they are trading, not just what setup they want.
