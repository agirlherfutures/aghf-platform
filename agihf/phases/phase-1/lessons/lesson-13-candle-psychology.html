Candlesticks Tell the Story

A candlestick is not just a colored block on the chart. It is a tiny story of what buyers and sellers did during that timeframe.

~15 minChart foundation+90 XP
Core idea

Candles show price behavior during a period of time.

Every candle has a beginning, an ending, a high point, and a low point. When you read candles correctly, you stop seeing random shapes and start seeing pressure, control, and movement.

Reinforce
A bullish candle does not mean buyers control the whole market forever. It means during that candle, buyers were able to close price higher than where it opened.
