Candle Psychology

Candle psychology is where the chart starts talking. You are no longer just seeing green and red candles. You are reading buyers, sellers, control, rejection, acceptance, and candle closures.

~18 minPsychology lesson+100 XP
Core idea

Every candle is a buyer and seller battle.

Buyers push price up. Sellers push price down. The candle close shows who held control by the end of that candle. The wick shows where price went but did not fully accept.

Teach → Experience

Who is in control?

Tap the candle that shows buyers had stronger control by the close. Do not just look at the color — look at where price closed.

Close = control clue
Buyer clueHigher close
Seller clueLower close
Main focusCandle close
Acceptance vs rejection

Did price accept or reject the level?

Price pushes above resistance. One example closes above it. The other wicks above but closes back below. Choose the example that shows acceptance above resistance.

Candle closes above resistance

Price pushed above and held the close.

Wick above, close below

Price reached above but did not hold.

Reinforce
A wick through a level is not the same as a candle close beyond that level. In your framework, the close gives the stronger clue of acceptance.
Support + resistance behavior

Buyers defend lows. Sellers defend highs.

Tap the correct read. Price comes into support, wicks down, then closes back above support. What does that show?

Random movement

The wick means nothing.

Buyer defense

Price dipped but buyers pushed it back up.

Clean breakdown

Support fully accepted below.

What you know now

⚔️
Every candle is a battle. Buyers push up. Sellers push down.
🕯️
Wicks show rejection or failed attempts. Price traveled there but did not close there.
✅
Closures show acceptance. The close tells you where price finished for that candle.
📍
Context matters. A candle near support or resistance tells a stronger story than a candle floating randomly.
