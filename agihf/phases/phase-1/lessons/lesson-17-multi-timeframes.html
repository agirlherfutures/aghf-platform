MTF Execution Mode

This is not another “what is a timeframe” lesson. This is where you build the trade from top to bottom: 4H bias → 1H structure → 1M entry. You cannot skip steps because skipping context is how traders get bullied by the small timeframe.

~22 minExecution lab+120 XP
Core idea

You do not guess entries. You build them.

The 4H gives the bias, the 1H gives the structure and levels, and the 1M gives the entry detail. Each layer unlocks the next one.
