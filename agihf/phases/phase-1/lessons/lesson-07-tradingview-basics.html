TradingView Basics
This is your workspace lesson. The goal is not to learn every button TradingView has. The goal is to know how to use it like a trader: open the chart, switch timeframes, and mark what matters.
~16 minHands-on chart lesson+80 XP
Core idea
TradingView is your workspace, not just a pretty chart.
You are using it to read price, change perspective with timeframes, and mark behavior so your eyes stop feeling lost.
Teach → Experience
Meet the workspace
Tap the tool pills and chart tools. This lesson keeps it simple: chart, timeframe, line tool, and horizontal level.

Detailed breakdown
The only TradingView tools beginners actually need first
Chart = where price lives.
Timeframe = how zoomed in or out you are.
Line tool = helps you connect structure or draw simple guides.
Horizontal level = helps you mark where price clearly reacted.

That’s enough to start reading the market like a real person instead of just scrolling and hoping.
